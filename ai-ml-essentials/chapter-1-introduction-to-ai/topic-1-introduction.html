🤖 Topic 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Examples include:

  • Speech Recognition
  • Image Recognition
  • Language Translation
  • Decision Making
  • Autonomous Vehicles

Definitions of AI

  • John McCarthy: AI is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines.
  • Stuart Russell: AI studies intelligent agents that perceive and act upon an environment.

Characteristics of AI

  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Problem Solving
  • Decision Making
  • Knowledge Representation
  • Natural Language Understanding

AI vs Traditional Programming

Traditional Programming Artificial Intelligence
Explicit Rules Learns from Data
Fixed Logic Adaptive Logic
Deterministic Probabilistic

Applications of AI

  • Healthcare
  • Education
  • Agriculture
  • Finance
  • Cyber Security
  • E-Commerce
  • Transportation

Key Takeaways

  • AI enables machines to mimic intelligent human behaviour.
  • AI systems learn, reason and make decisions.
  • AI is transforming almost every industry.
