🤖 Topic 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

1. What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of Computer Science that aims to develop systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem solving, decision making, perception and language understanding.

2. Definitions of AI

  • John McCarthy: "AI is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines."
  • Stuart Russell and Peter Norvig: AI is the study of intelligent agents that perceive their environment and take actions to achieve goals.

3. Characteristics of AI

  • Learning from experience
  • Reasoning and decision making
  • Problem solving
  • Knowledge representation
  • Natural language processing
  • Pattern recognition
  • Adaptability

4. AI vs Traditional Programming

Traditional Programming Artificial Intelligence
Uses predefined rules Learns from data
Produces fixed outputs Can improve over time
Suitable for structured problems Suitable for complex problems

5. Real-World Applications of AI

  • Virtual Assistants (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant)
  • Recommendation Systems (Netflix, YouTube, Amazon)
  • Healthcare Diagnosis
  • Fraud Detection in Banking
  • Autonomous Vehicles
  • Smart Agriculture
  • Chatbots and Generative AI

6. Key Takeaways

  • AI enables machines to mimic intelligent human behaviour.
  • AI systems can learn, reason and make decisions.
  • AI has applications across almost every industry.
  • AI is one of the most transformative technologies of the modern era.
